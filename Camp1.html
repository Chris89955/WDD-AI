Summer Camp WDD Using AI

Welcome to the WDD AI Summer Camp

The WDD AI Summer Camp is an exciting opportunity for students to learn about web design and development using artificial intelligence. Participants will engage in hands-on projects, interactive sessions, and collaborative learning experiences.

What You Will Learn

  • Basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Introduction to AI and its applications in web development
  • Building responsive and interactive web pages
  • Collaborative project work

Camp Details

Date: July 15 - July 30, 2024

Location: Online
